MARCH 2018

The Power of Play and Participation.

In 2012, as part of our ongoing commitment to grassroots philanthropy and educational support, Inyathelo visited Sheik Anta Diop college. This wasn't just another official site visit; it was a vibrant celebration of the energy and potential within our local schools..

About the Event

The playground was transformed as students from across the grades came together for a day of interactive activities and shared learning. Whether it was through song, dance, or organized games, the focus remained on building a sense of belonging and pride within the school community

Why Schools Like Sheik Anta Diop Matter

» Foster Leadership: Encouraging students to take an active role in their school culture..

» Strengthen Bonds: Connecting the school more deeply with local community resources..

» Celebrate Achievement: Recognizing the hard work of teachers and students alike in a joyful, informal setting.

Looking Back to Move Forward

Reflecting on this 2012 event reminds us of the lasting impact that a single day of focused attention and community spirit can have. We remain inspired by the smiles of the Sheik Anta Diop learners and continue to advocate for every child's right to a vibrant, supportive, and well-resourced educational journey.?
