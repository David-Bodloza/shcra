The Value of Active Play

Encourage Discipline: Teaching the importance of rules, practice, and perseverance. Build Confidence: Giving every child a chance to shine and be part of a winning team Strengthen Community: Using sports as a universal language to bring students together across different backgrounds

Overview

The highlight of the day was an interactive sports session where students swapped their pens for a soccer ball. Guided by local mentors, the children learned that teamwork on the field translates to collaboration in the classroom and the community.

What to Expect

  • Active Engagement: This is not a "sit and listen" event. Expect high-energy drills, friendly matches, and hands-on coaching..
  • Skill Development: Beyond soccer techniques, students will practice communication, strategic thinking, and mutual respect.
  • Mentorship: Access to positive role models who demonstrate how discipline and a positive attitude lead to success.
  • Inclusive Fun: An environment where every child, regardless of skill level, is encouraged to participate and feel part of the team.

A Legacy of Support

Reflecting on 2012 reminds us that small interventions—like a new soccer ball or an afternoon of guided play—leave a lasting impression. At Bez Valley Primary, we didn't just see a game; we saw the future of South Africa’s vibrant democracy in action.
