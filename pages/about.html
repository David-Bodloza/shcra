About SHCRA

Our association is dedicated to serving refugees with love, compassion, and practical care. We believe every person deserves dignity and a new beginning.

Our Vision

Rooted in both local and refugee communities values, we walk alongside those displaced by conflict and hardship,offering spiritual guidance, shelter, education, and emotional support.

We believe that every individual deserves safety, dignity, and the opportunity to rebuild their life, regardless of their background. Our work is driven by compassion, guided by Christian principles, and empowered by a global community of supporters and volunteers.

Our Mission

To assist, train and inform refugees and local community members so that they can become self-sufficient.

To help needy and disadvantaged people, refugees and local communities to sustain themselves.

Objectives

To help needy and disadvantaged people, refugees and local communities to sustain themselves.

Our Journey

The Genesis of Compassion

In 2015, driven by the escalating refugee crisis, a small group of committed individuals came together with a shared faith and an urgent desire to help. This marked the informal beginning of Christian Refugees Aid, operating initially as a grassroots volunteer effort.

  • Informal volunteer network established.
  • Initial focus on direct humanitarian aid.
  • Community support meetings began.

Formalization and Expansion

Recognizing the growing need, Christian Refugees Aid officially registered as a non-profit organization in 2017. This allowed for greater fundraising capabilities, expanded partnerships, and the ability to offer more structured programs, including initial legal aid clinics.

  • Official non-profit registration achieved.
  • Partnerships with local churches and NGOs.
  • Launched first legal aid program.

Building a Future of Hope

Today, we proudly serve as a vital lifeline for countless Christian refugees, offering comprehensive integration programs, robust spiritual support, and ongoing humanitarian assistance. Our commitment to empowering individuals and fostering resilient communities remains unwavering.

  • Expanded to international partnerships.
  • Launched vocational training initiatives.
  • Advocacy for refugee rights on a global scale.
