Our Services

Providing holistic support for refugees and South Africans in need.

🚑

Health

We help with Health and Counselling to refugees and citizens of South Africa

  • HIV/AIDS and STDs awareness campaign
  • Clinic and Hospital support
  • Trauma-informed counseling
🏠

Emergency Assistance

We assist clothing and food, temporary accommodations.

  • Accommodation
  • Food and clothing distribution
📚

Education and Training

Ensuring refugees are educated and training for work or entrepreneurship.

  • Education cost for children
  • Skills and Development
  • Job creation for youth and adults
🤝

Reconciliation

Breaking the cycle of conflict between refugees themselves and citizens

  • Listen with empathy.
  • Confess and take responsibility
  • Determine what needs to be done to make amends
