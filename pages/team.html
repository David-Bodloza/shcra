Meet Our Team

Mr Lukozi Bulimwengo

Program Director

With over 20 years in non-profit management, Mr Lukozi oversees our strategic vision and ensures that every program aligns with our mission of refugee empowerment and self-help.

Contact Mr Lukozi

Dr Wiya Leon Masudi

Secretary

Having worked with the organization for over 10 years, Dr Wiya Léon Masudi is a specialized researcher affiliated with the Environmental Biotechnology Rhodes University (EBRU). His work primarily focuses on bioremediation and the rehabilitation of lands degraded by mining activities, utilizing consortia of fungi and rhizobacteria.

Contact Dr Wiya

Mrs Ikosi Zaina

Accountant

Since 2011, Ikosi Zaina has been an integral part of the SHCRA family. Her professional journey is defined by a commitment to service, having previously worked in the field of education. An accounting professional by training, Ikosi uses her financial expertise to support our mission and ensure our continued growth.

Contact Mrs Zaina

Mr Omari Katumbus

IT Hardware Enginner(Board Member)

Mr Omari is an IT Hardware Engineer and a dedicated Board Member for the Self Help Christian Refugee Association (SHCRA). With a strong background in hardware design and troubleshooting, they bring a unique technical lens to the board, focusing on innovation and vocational empowerment. At SHCRA, Mr Omari is passionate about using technology to bridge the skills gap, helping refugees and local community members achieve peaceful integration and socioeconomic independence.

Contact Mr Omari

Mrs Odia Mbuyi

Social Worker (Board Member)

Mrs Mbuyi is a professional Social Worker and a Board Member for the Self Help Christian Refugee Association (SHCRA). Bringing a wealth of experience in social welfare and human rights, they advocate for a "people-first" approach to refugee assistance. At SHCRA, Mrs Mbuyi focuses on empowering individuals through holistic social support and training, helping them move from dependency to self-reliance within the South African society.
