Our Organization

Built on Faith and Resiliance

SHCRA was founded in 2000 on the belief that refugees are not just recipients of aid, but agents of change. Upon arriving in South Africa we found that no organization was ready to welcome the refugees, we had to rely on our selves for essentials like food, accommodation and education Most of refugees here in South Africa, came to the country, empty handed with no acknowledgment.

Our Core Focus

🎯

Mission

To assist, train, and to inform refugees and local community members so that they become self-sufficient.

👁️

Vision

To help needy refugees and local Community members to sustain themselves.

📉

Objective

to facilitate a peaceful integration of refugees in South African community and to improve the socio-economic living conditions of needy people regardless of religion, race,gender,tribe,nationaliies or ethnic groups.
