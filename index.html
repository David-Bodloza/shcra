Our Mission

SHCRA is dedicated to assisting those in needy and who have fled persecution, offering comprehensive support, community integration, and spiritual guidance to help them rebuild their lives in safety and dignity.

We believe in the power of faith and community to heal and empower. Our commitment is to walk alongside those displaced, providing a beacon of hope in their journey towards a brighter future.
